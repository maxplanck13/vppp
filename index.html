Our Roots & Vision

Learn about our purpose and our responsibility to the planet.

About Us

Located in Koothappar - 620014, Tamil Nadu, India, VPACK PULP PRODUCTS specializes in providing high-quality, sustainable, and eco-friendly paper egg boxes designed to protect and preserve eggs securely during transportation and storage.

Using meticulously recycled materials and biodegradable pulp, our egg cartons offer an environmentally responsible alternative to plastic packaging. Whether you operate a small family farm, a local grocery chain, or a large packaging supplier, our custom-designed paper egg boxes are heavily tailored to meet your physical needs.

Our Vision

Our commitment to innovation and sustainability has made us a highly trusted name in the agricultural packaging industry. We firmly believe in a zero-waste future where industrial shipping and local farming logistics rely completely on nature-based solutions.

We supply a wide range of sizes and custom configurations to cater directly to businesses of all scales. By maintaining a focus on premium quality and minimal environmental impact, we aim to lead the way back to sustainable, farm-friendly packaging solutions.
